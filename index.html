Our Menu

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced tomato-based gravy, flavored with butter and aromatic spices.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful South Asian dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of spices, layered and cooked together for a rich, fragrant one-pot meal.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a South Indian dish consisting of a thin, crispy fermented rice and lentil crepe filled with a spiced potato masala, typically served with chutney and sambar.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with marinated, grilled chicken simmered in a buttery tomato-based sauce, flavored with mild spices.
